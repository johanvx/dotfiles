# Rewrite Checklist (Signal, Flow, Integrity)

## Signal
- [ ] One takeaway is explicit.
- [ ] Every paragraph supports that takeaway.
- [ ] Vague sentences are rewritten or removed.

## Flow
- [ ] Paragraph order is logical.
- [ ] Transitions are explicit where needed.
- [ ] Sentence rhythm is concise and readable.

## Integrity
- [ ] No invented facts or numbers.
- [ ] Weak claims are tagged `[EVIDENCE NEEDED]`.
- [ ] Uncertain details are tagged `[VERIFY DETAIL]`.

## Compression
- [ ] Repetition removed.
- [ ] Filler/intensifiers reduced.
- [ ] Final close is concrete and bounded.
